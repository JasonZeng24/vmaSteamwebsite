About Our Department

Shaping Future Technologists Since 2015

The VMA STEAM & Computer Science Department prepares students to lead in the digital age. Through a vertically-aligned curriculum — from introductory programming to AP-level coursework — we build strong foundations in computational thinking, problem-solving, and engineering design.

Our program covers the full spectrum: software development, artificial intelligence, robotics, web technologies, cybersecurity, and digital fabrication. Students develop both technical skills and the collaborative, ethical mindset required to make meaningful impact.
